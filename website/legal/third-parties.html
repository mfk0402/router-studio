Software updates (desktop builds)

Packaged releases may use electron-updater to check for newer versions from your release host (for example GitHub Releases). That check uses the update metadata you configure at build time — review the Electron Builder / publisher documentation for your distribution.

Fonts (this website)

Marketing pages may load Inter and JetBrains Mono from Google Fonts when viewed in a browser. Self-host fonts if your deployment policy requires it.
